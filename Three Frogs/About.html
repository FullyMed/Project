    About Three Frogs Boardgame

    Who We Are

    Three Frogs Boardgame Surabaya is a cozy space where friends and families come together to enjoy over 100 exciting board games.

    Why "Three Frogs"?

    Inspired by three friends who shared a passion for board games and late-night laughter, Three Frogs was founded as a fun escape from the digital world.

    Our Mission

    To bring people together through fun, face-to-face gaming experiences and build a friendly boardgame community.

    Services

    • Boardgame rental & play area
    • Private table booking
    • Events & boardgame nights
